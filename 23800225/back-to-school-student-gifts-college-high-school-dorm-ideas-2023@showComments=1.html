The Verge’s 2023 back-to-school gift guide

From laptops and pens to consoles and streaming devices, here are the best supplies to help you or the student in your life prepare for the year ahead.

By Sheena Vasani, a writer covering commerce, e-readers, and tech news. She previously wrote about everything from web development to AI at Inside.

Autumn is coming, which means that back-to-school season is nearly upon us whether we like it or not. And as surprising — and possibly panic-inducing — as it is to hear, that also means that it’s time to start prepping for the inevitable return to the classroom. Thankfully, with the right supplies, you can ensure you or your loved one kicks off the school year on the right foot.

With the help of the Verge staff, we’ve collected a range of items intended to help the student in your life succeed. They include staples that will last them long after graduation, like the 13-inch MacBook Air, as well as gifts designed to help them recharge between classes, from Nintendo’s Zelda-inspired Switch OLED to Codenames. We even have a gift that will allow them to capture memories in a fun, creative way.

Feel free to peruse our picks below, all of which should make the 2023-2024 school year one to remember.
