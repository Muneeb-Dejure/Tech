The Verge’s 2022 New Year’s resolution gift guide

We’ve assembled a collection of some of our favorite tech to help you commit to improving yourself in the new year.

By Alice Jovanée

Dec 27, 2022, 4:11 PM UTC

Share this story

If you buy something from a Verge link, Vox Media may earn a commission. See our ethics statement.

The end of the year is a perfect time to reflect, take inventory of past accomplishments, and think about how you can better yourself and others in 2023. Staying committed to a New Year’s resolution even through February can be a tall order, though, which is why we’ve collected some terrific products to keep you — or someone else you know — motivated throughout the year.

Some of our picks this year include the latest Kindle Paperwhite ($140) and Apple Watch Series 8 ($399) so your giftee can channel their inner bookworm or keep closer tabs on the litany of fitness goals they’ve undoubtedly set for the new year. We’ve also included some less tech-centric items, like a 32-ounce Hydro Flask ($37) as well as a reliable pair of hiking shoes from Danner ($190) that will help you make the most of the great outdoors whenever you manage to escape the city.

Regardless of how big your 2023 goals are, our gift guide has something to get you excited about them — whether you’re trying to be a healthier human, be kinder to the environment, or just get your life in order.

Time is a precious commodity, which is why proper budgeting is of the utmost importance. Thankfully, Time Timer's simple Home Mod lets your giftee set a 60-minute countdown with a simple turn of the dial. The colorful timer is perfect for those who use the Pomodoro method to stay focused on their work at home or just any time you want to put a hard limit on how long you spend on a given task.

Dymo LetraTag 100H Handheld Label Maker

If you know someone who is looking to get organized or cut down on clutter, the Dymo LetraTag 100H label maker can help. Powered by four AA batteries, the handheld device features a 26-character keypad with a variety of style and formatting options, along with an LCD display to preview labels before your print them. The LetraTag can also store up to nine labels, just in case there's anything you need to tag frequently.
Price: $20.99

iFixit Pro Tech Toolkit

Exercise your right to repair in the new year with the iFixit Pro Tech Toolkit. The self-contained toolkit not only comes packaged with 64 precision screwdriver bits but also plenty of other handy tools to repair everything from eyeglasses to iPhones. Best of all, every Pro Tech Toolkit is covered by a warranty that will replace any tool that breaks over the lifetime of the product.

The fact it supports USB-C is just an added plus.
