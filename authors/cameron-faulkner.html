Cameron Faulkner

Writer

Cameron Faulkner is a writer at The Verge, focused on reviews, news, and deals coverage. He’s the guy responsible for covering all of the monitors, webcams, and gaming headsets that are worth your attention. However, you’ll find his byline on posts about VR, laptops, TVs, battery packs, and a lot more.

Before joining The Verge in 2018, he worked at TechRadar as the associate mobile editor, covering everything to do with phones. He got his break writing online in 2012 as an intern at the now-defunct news branch of AOL’s Games.com.

When he’s not at work, Cameron’s usually gaming, playing the drums, trying to cook, and playing with cats, sometimes simultaneously.
