The Verge’s 2022 fall gift guide

Our fall gift guide includes plenty of ideas for enjoying the turning of the season, whether you want to admire it outside or bundled up at home.

By Alice Jovanée

Updated Oct 13, 2022, 5:04 PM UTC

Share this story

The weather is getting cooler and the days shorter, which means fall has arrived, and we’re paving the road to Halloween with our spookiest gift guide of the year. It’s time to grab your black cat, a fistful of candy corn, and dive into our unapologetically autumnal gift guide.

Naturally, with all of the new tech that’s just rolled out, we couldn’t help but include recent picks like Google’s midrange Pixel 6A ($349) and the second-gen AirPods Pro ($239). However, we’ve also collected a host of more affordable options if you’re trying to reign in your budget, like Anker’s 30W 511 Charger ($22.99) and a behemoth Thermos ($43.50) that’s as suitable for hot chocolate as it is spiced apple cider.

We’ve even collected a number of gifts that are less tech-centric, such as a stainless steel fire pit ($264.99), a heated plushie ($38), and a macabre card game ($27.95) that can make spending time with your family — chosen or otherwise — a little more enjoyable.

Hopefully, our roundup sparks your imagination if you’re having trouble finding the perfect gift this fall, whether it be for a birthday, wedding, or another festive occasion.
